Chicken Broccoli and Rice

Chicken, broccoli, and rice? Yes, that's right. Every body builder's go-to meal.

This right here, is a meal that will never let you down. Satiating. Quick and easy Preparation.
Delicious. Oh, did I forget to mention it's also healthy? A one stop shop for all your macro needs.
Right here in one dish.

Ingredients

  • Thawed Chicken Breast
  • Uncooked Short Grain Rice
  • Broccoli (Frozen or Fresh)
  • Olive Oil
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • Hot Sauce (If Preferred)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Cover chicken with a thin layer of olive oil.
  3. Apply chicken seasoning, covering the entirety of the breast.
  4. Place in oven on baking sheet for about 30 minutes or until fully cooked.
  5. Place rice in rice cooker untl done (usually takes 30 minutes).
  6. Steam broccoli
  7. Once chicken, broccoli and rice are all done, combine onto your favorite plate.
  8. Apply hot sauce if you deem it necessary.
