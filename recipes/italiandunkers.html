Italian Dunkers

What is this delicious looking specimen?

You take crispy baked bread and dip it in a hot and meaty sauce.
Does it get any better than that? Here's a step-by-step on how to prepare it!

Ingredients

  • Fresh Italian Bread
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesean
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cut bread in half, spread butter and then sprinkle parmesean generously.
  3. Insert bread into the oven for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook beef in a medium fryig pan until its medium
  5. Add marinara sauce to the cooked beef. Keep heat on medium.
  6. Pour meaty sauce into a bowl and start dipping away!
