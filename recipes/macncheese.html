Macaroni and Cheese

Is it just me, or is your mouth wet too?

Be taken aback by this wonderful dish made from tender penne pasta cooked to al dente.
Served with creamy, gooey mozzarella and smoked gouda.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Smoked Gouda Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta to al dente.
  2. Shock the pasta by running cold water over
  3. Make a roux out of the flour, butter and the milk.
  4. Add grated cheese after the roux has been made.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
